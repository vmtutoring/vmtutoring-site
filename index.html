ABOUT

Group Sessions

Group tutoring sessions are the "sweet spot" in between our private tutoring and standard classroom instruction. Group tutors can give more attention to students in a small group than they would in a large classroom, but there are still enough students to keep everyone engaged. Students may be inspired by others as they watch each other work through problems and support each other, since every student brings something new to the group.

Personalized Solutions

We offer private tutoring services that are personally tailored to your learning needs. Every student is different and we formulate individual learning plans for each according to different situations and according to their preferences and aptitude. We not only encourage faster learning progress, but we also strive to cultivate and establish good living and learning habits.

Experienced Tutors

Not only will they patiently and passionately work with each student, but they will actively monitor your child's progress and offer the perfect learning solution for everybody. All of our tutors come from a strong educational background and we have teamed up at VM Tutoring because we all have a common goal. We provide our tutors with the opportunity to connect to something bigger, supporting our students every step of the way as they learn and grow.
